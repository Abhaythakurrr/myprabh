1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our service.

MyPrabh Cookie Information:

  • Website: aiprabh.com

  • Contact: abhay@aiprabh.com
  • Operator: Abhay

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Authentication Cookies: Remember your login status
  • Form Cookies: Remember form data during your session

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Google Analytics: Track website usage and performance
  • User Behavior: Understand how users interact with features
  • Performance Metrics: Monitor website speed and functionality
  • Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical issues

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience on our website:

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Language Cookies: Remember your language selection
  • Theme Cookies: Remember your display preferences
  • Chat Cookies: Maintain AI companion conversation context

Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us show you relevant content:

  • Social Media: Enable social media sharing features
  • Advertising: Show relevant ads on other websites
  • Retargeting: Show MyPrabh ads to previous visitors
  • Conversion Tracking: Measure marketing campaign effectiveness

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Maintain your login session and account security

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior patterns
  • Improve our service based on usage data
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

5. Cookie Duration

Different cookies have different lifespans:

Session Cookies

  • Deleted when you close your browser
  • Used for temporary data during your visit
  • Essential for website functionality

Persistent Cookies

  • Remain on your device for a set period
  • Remember your preferences between visits
  • Typically expire after 30 days to 2 years
  • Can be deleted manually by you

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you can:

  • Accept all cookies for the full experience
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

Opt-Out Options

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Social Media: Adjust settings in your social media accounts
  • Marketing: Contact us to opt out of marketing cookies
