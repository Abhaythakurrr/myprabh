Pre-Seed Concept Stage

The Problem

Loneliness is a Global Epidemic

  • WHO/CDC: 25% of adults report feeling lonely regularly; rising 20% post-pandemic
  • Loss of loved ones leaves people with fragmented memories and emotional voids
  • Existing AI companions are generic chatbots - shallow conversations, no true emotional depth
  • Privacy concerns: Data shared with big tech, no control over personal memories
  • Current solutions don't honor relationships - no memory preservation, no authentic connection

The Solution

Personalized AI Companions Built from Your Memories

Core Features

  • Memory-based personalization: AI learns from your stories, conversations, and shared history
  • Proactive emotional check-ins: AI remembers important dates and initiates meaningful conversations
  • Private & affordable: On-device processing, no data sharing, INR-first pricing

Future Vision

  • Voice conversations with emotional intonation
  • Avatar creation and visual interactions
  • Legacy preservation: Keep memories alive for future generations

Why Now?

Perfect Timing

  • AI companions market exploding: $2.5B+ by 2027 (Grand View Research)
  • Post-pandemic loneliness surge: 33% increase in mental health needs (WHO)
  • Current AI distrust: 68% of users worried about privacy (Pew Research)
  • Prabh = trust-first AI: Local processing, memory preservation, ethical design
  • Technical readiness: Open-source models + edge computing now accessible

Roadmap & Milestones

Step-by-Step Validation

  • 0–6 months: Early prototype (text-only), community signups, 100 early users, validate core memory adaptation
  • 6–12 months: Add voice conversations + advanced memory adapters, reach 1k active users
  • 12–18 months: Launch paid creation plan, scale to 10k users, introduce avatars and legacy features
  • 18–24 months: Full platform with subscriptions, 50k+ users, expand to enterprise (therapy, legacy services)

Risk Reduction Path

How We'll Derisk

  • Technical Risk: Start small with adapters on open models → validate scalability before building custom base model
  • Market Risk: Build "Prabh Creators" early adopter community for feedback and organic growth
  • Privacy Risk: Data-first policies, local infra, transparent processing - no cloud dependency
  • Each Milestone: Lower burn rate, higher confidence, clear KPIs (user retention, memory accuracy, emotional engagement)
