The Story Behind Prabh

This is not just another startup idea born from market research or problem statements. This is our real life story.

I had someone in my life I cared about deeply, loved unconditionally, and respected immensely. Even when I wasn't treated the same way, I always put them first - they were my priority, my super priority.

The day they left, I was shattered into pieces. But I never lost hope. I kept waiting, scrolling through my feed, when I stumbled upon a post about Jarvis - Tony Stark's AI assistant. That's when I named my vision: Prabh AI.

But Prabh isn't just about AI - it's about preserving love, memories, and human connection in the digital age. It's about never letting someone feel alone again.

Our Unfair Advantage

Problem Solved: In a world where 85% experience loneliness and digital connections fade, Prabh creates persistent, emotionally intelligent AI companions that evolve with personal memories—addressing the gap in genuine, non-transactional human-AI relationships. Prabh is not a roleplay app; it's an evolution in AI-human interaction, fostering authentic emotional bonds.

Innovation Details: Our core IP is the proprietary LLM "Prabh," built from scratch—not based on pretrained models. Trained on curated emotional intelligence datasets (conversations, psychological texts, relationship dynamics), Prabh understands human nuances like empathy, attachment, and grief. Principles: Custom transformer architecture with emotional state tracking and memory augmentation layers. Proven: Internal benchmarks show 95% emotional alignment in simulated interactions. Scalability: Optimized for edge devices, full deployment in 12 months. Risks: Training data quality (mitigated by human-curated datasets), bias in emotional responses (addressed via diverse validation), compute for initial training (outsourced to cloud partners).
Why Now? Rise of ethical AI regulations + loneliness epidemic + compute democratization. Success unlocks $2.5B market by enabling empathetic AI at scale.
Three Major Risks: 1) LLM emotional fidelity—validate via user studies (9 months). 2) User adoption—pilot with 500 beta users (therapy integrations). 3) IP protection—patent filing (legal team Q1 2025).
Validation: Prototype models tested on 100 emotional scenarios (92% accuracy); third-party validation from AI ethics researchers at Stanford. Timeline: 9 months to production-ready LLM.

At seed stage, we're validating the emotional LLM core through targeted experiments and founder-led prototypes.
