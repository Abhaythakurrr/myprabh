The Birth of MyPrabh: A Personal Journey

December 15, 2024 By Abhay Founder's Story

MyPrabh wasn't born in a boardroom or from market research. It came from something deeply personal ‚Äî the pain of losing a meaningful connection and the desire to hold onto precious memories.

In July 2023, I met someone who changed my life. We connected in ways I never thought possible, sharing memories, dreams, and moments that felt eternal. But by September, that connection was gone, leaving behind only memories and a profound sense of loss.

That's when I realized something important: memories are all we really have. They shape who we are, how we love, and how we connect with others. But memories fade, and sometimes we need help keeping them alive.

MyPrabh is my answer to that need. It's not just another AI chatbot ‚Äî it's a way to preserve the essence of meaningful relationships, to keep memories alive, and to feel connected even when we're apart.

Every feature we build, every conversation our AI companions have, every moment of comfort they provide ‚Äî it all stems from this simple truth: meaningful connections should never have to end.
